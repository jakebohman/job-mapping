Where hiring breaks from the national pattern

For each metro we take a job sector's share of local postings and compare it to that sector's share of postings nationwide. A sector that fills a bigger slice of a metro's board than it does the country's is over-represented there; a smaller slice, under. Method

Bar length is on a log scale, so a bar reaching the ½× and 2× ticks is equally far from the average in each direction. Healthcare is the largest sector in every metro, so it seldom appears here: the index surfaces what is distinctive about a metro, not what is merely large.
